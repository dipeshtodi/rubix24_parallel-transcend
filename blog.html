Government Schemes

The government has implemented targeted schemes to support disabled individuals. The Assistance Program provides financial aid, education, vocational training, and specialized healthcare. Inclusive employment incentives aim to enhance job opportunities. Healthcare schemes offer affordable medical care and assistive devices. These initiatives collectively aim to improve the quality of life, economic independence and social inclusion.

Scholarships & Fundings

The government & private organization's Scholarship and Funding Program offers diverse initiatives to support education. It includes scholarships covering tuition and materials, financial aid for economically challenged students, and grants for research and innovation. These efforts aim to make education accessible, recognizing and alleviating financial barriers for individuals pursuing academic excellence.

        

Government Exams

The Indian Government Exams Support Program is designed to aid individuals preparing for government exams. This initiative includes coaching scholarships to cover tuition costs, financial assistance for economically disadvantaged candidates, and special grants for research and development in exam preparation methodologies. The program strives to ensure equal opportunities for all, fostering accessibility and success in government examinations. There's reserved quota seats for specially abled people to work in adminstration of Government.

NGOs Support for Healthcare

NGOs are instrumental in providing healthcare support for individuals with disabilities, aiming to enhance their quality of life and, when possible, address disabilities through surgical interventions. These organizations focus on offering specialized medical services, including surgeries and rehabilitation, tailored to the unique needs of disabled individuals.
